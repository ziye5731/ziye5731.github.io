This page lists some of the projects that I am currently working on or have completed.

### FineTune
FineTune is an open-source project that focuses on 3D-printed accessories/tools/machines for double-reed instruments, which aims to decrease the cost of playing the oboe and the bassoon, and promote innovations in reed studies.

Links: [Project page](https://github.com/finetune-double-reeds).
